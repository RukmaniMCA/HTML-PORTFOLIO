The Best Movies According to Rukmani

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Forrest Gump

The character of Forrest Gump believes that good things can happen for him and dares to go after them. This funny yet inspiring story is among the best motivational movies and teaches you how you can achieve great heights only if you reach for them.


Dangal

The film is all about women's power and how people need to change their thinking that girls can only do household work.


I am Kalam

I am Kalam is a movie about hope and self-belief. The movie depicts the life of a poor boy, Kalam who works in a local tea shop.
He dreams of going to school wearing a uniform and learning about the world but is victim to his circumstances. The film is optimistic and inspires you to face all odds with a smile.
